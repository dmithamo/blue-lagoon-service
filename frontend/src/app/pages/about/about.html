About Blue Lagoon

Blue Lagoon Hotels and Resorts is a premier tourist destination located along the scenic Nanyuki-Meru Road. We pride ourselves as the destination of choice for both local and international tourists.

Our facility offers a unique blend of luxury accommodation, guided nature walks, and recreational gaming activities. Whether you're heading to Mt. Kenya or seeking a peaceful retreat, Blue Lagoon is your ideal destination.

With growing demand for our services, we've launched an online reservation system to make booking your stay easier than ever. Our modern REST API ensures seamless integration with your travel plans.
